Support

If you need help with NatHub or want to suggest a new experiment, contact:

saechoe09@gmail.com

Helpful Details

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • Your NatHub app version
  • The experiment where the issue appeared
  • The steps that led to the issue
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual or interaction-related

Launch Version

The launch version of NatHub does not require an account, does not include advertising, and does not include paid unlocks or online services.

支持

如果你在使用 NatHub 时遇到问题，或希望提出新的实验想法，请发送邮件至：

saechoe09@gmail.com

问题反馈信息

建议在邮件中包含：

  • 设备型号
  • iOS 版本
  • NatHub 版本
  • 出现问题的实验名称
  • 复现步骤
  • 如果是视觉或交互问题，截图会很有帮助

首发版本

NatHub 首发版本不要求注册账号，不包含广告，也不包含付费解锁或在线服务。
